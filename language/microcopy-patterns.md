# Microcopy Patterns

## CTA patterns
- Primary CTA: action-first and specific, for example `Get heart-healthy recipes` or `Donate today`.
- Secondary CTA: lower-pressure but still directional, for example `Explore support resources`.
- Tertiary CTA: utility or learning, for example `Read the guide`.

## Form guidance
- Labels should be explicit and calm.
- Error states should explain what to fix in plain language.
- Success states should confirm what happened and what comes next.

## Navigation guidance
- Prefer short, literal labels over clever naming.
- Use helper text where a category name alone may feel too broad.
