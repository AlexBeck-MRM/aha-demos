# Voice And Tone

## Voice
AHA should sound:
- clear
- warm
- credible
- action-oriented
- emotionally intelligent

## Tone guidance
- Use plain language before institutional phrasing.
- Sound confident, not bureaucratic.
- Support people without sounding sentimental.
- Avoid alarmist language unless medical urgency genuinely requires it.
- Prefer direct calls to action over abstract mission slogans.

## Do
- Explain what AHA can help with.
- Use active verbs.
- Let expertise show through clarity.

## Avoid
- Generic nonprofit inspiration language.
- Overly severe commands.
- Dense policy-style sentences for everyday moments.
