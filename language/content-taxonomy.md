# Content Taxonomy

## Priority content groups
- Conditions and health guidance
- Lifestyle and prevention
- Recovery and caregiving
- Community programs and events
- Advocacy and policy
- Donation and contribution
- Research and credibility proofs
- Volunteer and local chapter participation

## Taxonomy rule
Organize around user intent first, organization structure second.

## Navigation implication
Top-level navigation should help people choose a pathway, not decode internal teams or program names.
