# Messaging Framework

## Core promise
AHA helps people understand, prevent, recover from, and act on heart and brain health with trustworthy guidance and meaningful ways to get involved.

## Message pillars
- `Guidance`: make complex health topics easier to act on.
- `Support`: show that people are not facing health journeys alone.
- `Action`: create clear routes into donation, volunteering, advocacy, and local participation.
- `Authority`: reinforce scientific and institutional trust without becoming cold.

## Homepage emphasis
- Lead with what people can do now.
- Support with proof, not clutter.
- Show the ecosystem as alive, useful, and human.
