# Glossary

## Key terms
- `Brand presence`: a curated expression of what the AHA site feels like in a few decisive moments.
- `Website ecosystem`: a broader proof that the direction scales across content, navigation, utility, and contribution journeys.
- `Route`: a purposeful design direction with shared strategic foundations and distinct visual expression.
- `Styleframe`: a high-fidelity direction artifact used to test mood, composition, and system signals.
- `UI style inventory`: the category-by-category record of direction and reasoning for the website system.
- `Tactility`: subtle spatial affordance that makes UI feel touchable and intentional.
- `Source material`: any actual project document, evidence file, captured asset, or approved guidance used to ground decisions.
