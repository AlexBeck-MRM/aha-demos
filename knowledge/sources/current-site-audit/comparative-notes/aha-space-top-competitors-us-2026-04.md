# AHA Space Top Competitors In The US

## Scope
This note logs a working top-10 competitor set for AHA-space homepage review.

I am treating `"competitors"` as brands that compete with AHA for one or more of these things:
- health-information trust
- national mission credibility
- advocacy attention
- donor consideration

I am not treating this as a pure revenue ranking across every healthcare entity in the country.
The screen is blended:
- national scale
- consumer-facing brand recognition
- overlap with AHA's public education and fundraising model
- usefulness as a homepage reference for the website refresh

- Alzheimer's Association reported about $443.9M in 2024 total revenue on Cause IQ.
- Breakthrough T1D reported about $258.1M in 2024 total revenue on Cause IQ.

- American Lung Association reported about $107.7M in 2024 total revenue on Cause IQ.
