## Direction
- The topic guide should be the canonical home.
- The topic guide should show:
  - guide promise
  - quick find routes
  - numbered chapter overview
  - chapter-level summaries
  - curated supporting articles, recipes, tools, and infographics
- Supporting pages should show:
  - breadcrumb back to the guide
  - `Part of Chapter XX` context
  - `More in this chapter`
  - related guide or condition links where useful
- Resource libraries should be secondary utility layers, not the main IA.

## Page roles
- `Topic Guide Overview`: front door and table of contents for the topic.
- `Chapter Section`: durable evergreen learning unit.
- `Supporting Article`: article, infographic, or explainer attached to one chapter.
- `Resource Library`: filtered support surface for recipes, tools, programs, downloads, and translated assets.
- `Quick Find Routes`: goal-based entry points for users who do not know the chapter names.
- `Connected Health Links`: cross-links to conditions and adjacent Healthy Living guides.

## Recommendation
- Use a numbered chapter sequence on the guide page.
- Keep persistent navigation chapter-only.
- Put article findability inside chapter shelves, quick find routes, search, and resource filters.
- Do not show every article in the main navigation.
- Merge basic evergreen articles into guide chapter content when they repeat the main lesson.
