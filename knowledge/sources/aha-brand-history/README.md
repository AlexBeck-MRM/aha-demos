# AHA Brand History

This source cluster captures the brand history of the American Heart Association as research material for the website refresh.

Contents:
- `index.md` for the quick retrieval summary
- `canonical-sources.md` for the source list and provenance notes
- `brand-history-report.md` for the full narrative report
- `visual-timeline.md` for the image-led history
- `images/` for locally saved reference assets

Use this folder when the work needs historical grounding around:
- the role AHA has played as a public brand
- logo and symbol evolution
- the meaning of red in the system
- the shift from science-led messaging to movement-led and emotionally-led messaging
- known agency and in-house stewardship around newer brand phases

Downloaded images are stored here for internal research reference. Keep the source links attached when reusing them.
