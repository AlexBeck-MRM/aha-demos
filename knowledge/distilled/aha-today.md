# AHA Today

## Snapshot
AHA needs a digital presence that feels more modern, more ownable, and more emotionally intelligent than a typical healthcare nonprofit site.

## Current strengths to preserve
- High trust and recognizability.
- Strong mission clarity around heart health, support, and action.
- A large content and program ecosystem that can prove authority when organized well.

## Current risks to address
- Generic healthcare-web patterns can flatten the brand.
- Dense utility can become noise if not shaped by hierarchy and tactility.
- Overuse of red or severe typography can make the experience feel clinical or relentless.

## Working implication
The refresh should not discard trust signals or utility depth. It should reorganize them into a brighter, warmer, more deliberate digital system.
