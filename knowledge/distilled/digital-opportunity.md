# Digital Opportunity

## Opportunity statement
The refresh can turn AHA's website into a clearer expression of brand, service, and action by combining art direction, system logic, and operational consistency.

## Strategic opportunities
- Make the brand feel more ownable through composition, tactility, and image treatment.
- Use white space and depth to create calm without making the site feel sparse.
- Make large content ecosystems feel intentionally curated.
- Build a reusable UI language that scales from homepage to article, donation, search, and program pages.
- Connect design decisions more directly to delivery through prompts, logs, and structured source material.

## Success signal
The refreshed ecosystem should feel more like a coherent digital brand environment and less like a collection of nonprofit web templates.
