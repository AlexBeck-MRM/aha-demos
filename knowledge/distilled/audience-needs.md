# Audience Needs

## Primary needs implied by current project direction
- Quick orientation and reassurance.
- Clear pathways into action, support, donation, community participation, and learning.
- Content systems that feel navigable rather than overwhelming.
- Trust cues that prove expertise without reading as institutional clutter.

## Experience requirements
- Strong first-load clarity.
- Obvious affordances on interactive elements.
- Calm but useful content density.
- Accessible reading hierarchy and clear navigation logic.

## Content implication
The site should speak to people in moments of need, curiosity, and motivation. It must support both emotional entry points and practical task completion.
