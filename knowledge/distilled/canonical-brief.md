# Canonical Brief

## Mission
Reimagine heart.org as a trusted companion that helps AHA build stronger relationships, earn trust, and drive action across a connected digital ecosystem.

## Experience ambition
`A trusted companion that empowers every person on their journey.`

## Non-negotiables
- Bright, white-first interface with warmth and spatial clarity.
- Floating navigation, not browser-edge attachment.
- Tactile, touchable components: buttons, tabs, cards, fields.
- Strategic red, not constant red saturation.
- Search and navigation must become materially better, not just prettier.
- The ecosystem must feel human, useful, and clearly branded.

## Design principles in shorthand
- Build trust.
- Operate intelligently.
- Be human.
- Less is more.
- Create frictionless experiences.
- Be the number-one guide for heart health.

## Current route stance
- Route A: warmer, more editorial, more companion-led.
- Route B: cleaner, more precise, more system-led.
- Keep both alive until component-level differences and evidence reads are stronger.

## Core opportunity areas
- Unified narrative and brand expression.
- Better search and discovery.
- Cleaner ecosystem connections across domains and journeys.
- Stronger content governance, templates, tagging, and authoring workflows.
- Better timing of engagement opportunities.

## Technology implications
- Recommended direction centers on composable architecture with SitecoreAI.
- Frontend recommendation is Next.js plus Content SDK, Storybook, and automated testing.
- Search, template maturity, tagging, and content operations are major experience constraints, not just technical details.
