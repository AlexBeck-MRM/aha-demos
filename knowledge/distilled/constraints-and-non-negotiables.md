# Constraints And Non-Negotiables

## Confirmed working non-negotiables
- Floating navigation works better than navigation attached to the browser edge.
- Buttons, tabs, cards, and fields should have subtle skeuomorphic tactility.
- Primary buttons should feel chunky, rounded, softly elevated, and compelling to click.
- Red should remain a signature accent and should be used strategically.
- Red-filled surfaces should use subtle gradients instead of flat fills.
- The palette should stay mostly white with warm support neutrals.
- Photography must feel specific to AHA and avoid generic stock energy.
- Illustrations should become more ownable, simpler, flatter, and lighter.
- Overuse of all caps makes the site feel severe.

## Working constraints
- Accessibility and trust cannot be traded away for expression.
- The styleframe structure should stay separate from final styling decisions.
- Two routes should remain meaningfully distinct while sharing a strategic base.

## Open constraint
The official brand-guideline source has not yet been ingested into this repo, so current non-negotiables are working-direction statements grounded in project summary and existing repo material.
