# AHA Narrative

## Core narrative
AHA should feel like a trusted guide that helps people act, not a distant institution broadcasting information.

## Narrative characteristics
- Human: centered on families, caregivers, recovery, prevention, clinicians, and local action.
- Useful: moves quickly from meaning to help, next steps, and clarity.
- Credible: grounded in science, programs, and community trust.
- Hopeful: expresses action and support without sentimentality.

## Narrative tension to manage
The brand should feel warm and emotionally resonant without drifting into soft-focus charity tropes or generic wellness language.
