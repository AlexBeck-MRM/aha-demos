# Knowledge Guidance

Prioritize source fidelity over elegant summaries.
When updating `knowledge/sources/`, add or revise grounded notes before rewriting distilled files.
When updating `knowledge/distilled/`, keep summaries traceable to source files and log major changes.

For raster PDFs, screenshots, or dense board exports:
- use overlapping crops instead of tight non-overlapping slices
- keep enough margin so text blocks, card boundaries, and connectors are not cut off
- recrop anything that may have truncated a sentence or merged adjacent cards

For weak or partially legible sources:
- label confidence explicitly
- separate direct readable content from theme-level interpretation
- run a second-pass accuracy review before treating the ingest as retrieval-safe
