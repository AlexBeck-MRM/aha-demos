# Image Prompt Template: Styleframe 01

## Subject block
Design a curated homepage-led AHA website direction showing a dominant hero, floating navigation, a small number of cards, an editorial moment, and one utility module.

## Expression block
Bright white-first canvas, warm neutrals, strategic red accents, tactile rounded buttons, soft depth, believable AHA-family-and-community photography, no generic healthcare stock feel.

## Composition block
Prioritize one commanding hero moment, clear CTA hierarchy, and one memorable branded detail.

## Constraints
- Avoid flat red slabs.
- Avoid edge-attached navigation.
- Avoid overpacked grids.
