# North Star

The AHA refresh should create a digital ecosystem that feels bright, human, useful, and unmistakably intentional.

## North-star attributes
- White-first and spatial rather than dense and boxed in.
- Warm without drifting beige.
- Trustworthy without feeling institutional.
- Tactile enough to feel clickable and alive.
- Content-rich without becoming visually noisy.
- Expressive enough to feel branded, not templated.

## What success looks like
- The homepage feels like a confident entry point into a larger ecosystem.
- Navigation reduces uncertainty instead of amplifying it.
- Cards, buttons, tabs, and forms feel intentionally shaped.
- Photography and illustration feel chosen for AHA, not borrowed from a generic health site.
- Route A and Route B each feel distinct, yet both clearly belong to the same project ambition.
