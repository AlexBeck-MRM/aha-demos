# Route B

## Position
Cleaner, more precise, and slightly more system-forward.

## Desired feel
- crisp
- modern
- efficient
- trustworthy
- controlled but still human

## Visual behavior
- brighter whites and cleaner separations
- tighter component geometry while retaining softness
- more explicit system rhythm
- stronger sense of operational clarity in dense modules

## Best use
Use Route B when the work needs to prove scale, operational confidence, or strong utility across complex ecosystems.

## Guardrails
- Do not let precision become cold.
- Do not flatten all expression into strict minimalism.
- Keep image and copy moments warm enough to preserve AHA humanity.
