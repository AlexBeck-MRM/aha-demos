# Route Comparison

## Shared foundation
- floating navigation
- bright white-first canvas
- strategic use of red
- tactile component language
- trust through clarity and usefulness

## Route A advantage
Stronger emotional resonance and editorial atmosphere.

## Route B advantage
Stronger operational clarity and scalable system discipline.

## Decision lens
Choose Route A when the brand needs more visible warmth and story energy.
Choose Route B when the system needs to prove precision, breadth, and navigation confidence.

## Current recommendation
Keep both routes alive. The project still benefits from having one warmer expression and one cleaner expression while the shared system foundation continues to solidify.

## Fast reference
See `route-deltas.md` for the component-level differences that should stay explicit during iteration.
