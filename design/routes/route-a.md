# Route A

## Position
Warmer, slightly more editorial, and more emotionally atmospheric.

## Desired feel
- human
- inviting
- softly spatial
- tactile
- brand-forward without being loud

## Visual behavior
- warmer neutrals
- more generous image moments
- softer transitions between modules
- a touch more breakout behavior from illustration or photography

## Best use
Use Route A when the work needs stronger emotional pull, a more editorial homepage presence, or a more visibly human point of view.

## Guardrails
- Do not let warmth turn beige or sleepy.
- Do not let editorial moments reduce utility.
- Keep utility modules as considered as the hero.
