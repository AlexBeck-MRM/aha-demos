# Route Deltas

## Purpose
Keep route differences explicit at the component and experience-behavior level so the brain does not waste tokens re-deriving them.

## Hero
- Route A: more atmospheric, editorial, human, and emotionally inviting.
- Route B: more direct, structured, and system-legible.

## Navigation
- Route A: softer framing, slightly more curated and branded.
- Route B: cleaner rhythm, sharper orientation, stronger wayfinding emphasis.

## Cards
- Route A: more image-led and story-friendly.
- Route B: more utility-led and grid-disciplined.

## Search
- Route A: supportive, question-led, confidence-building.
- Route B: efficient, explicit, tool-like, and structured.

## Motion
- Route A: softer reveals and a little more atmosphere.
- Route B: quicker, more functional transitions.

## Tone
- Route A: companion-led.
- Route B: guide-and-system-led.

## Shared rule
Neither route should become generic healthcare minimalism or sentimental nonprofit softness.
