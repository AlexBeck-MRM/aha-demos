## Read
- Early spatial passes tested how much dimensionality the icon style could carry before becoming too realistic, textured, or cute.
- The colour-block pilot identified the accepted core behavior: broad matte masses, soft close shadows, neutral-first colour, red as a separated signal, and useful detail only where it clarifies meaning.
- The 12-sheet `v1-v3` files are pre-acceptance refinement passes. They should stay review-only.
- The accepted base sheet is `aha-medical-soft-spatial-colour-block-12-sheet-v4.png` in `design/generated/icons/medical-soft-spatial-colour-block/`.
