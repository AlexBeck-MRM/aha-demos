# Image Cropping

## Direction
Crop imagery for closeness, specificity, and emotional readability. Let crops feel intentional and occasionally bold, while preserving enough context to stay believable.

## Reasoning
Image cropping is one of the fastest ways to move away from generic stock behavior. Strong crops also help the site feel more editorial and human.

## Guardrail
Avoid awkward truncation, forced face crops, or compositions that feel sensationalized.
