# Mega Menu

## Direction
Treat the mega menu as a guided orientation surface, not a dense sitemap dump. Use clear topic groupings, one short descriptor per link, and a small amount of illustration so the drawer feels warm, legible, and easier to choose from.

## Reasoning
- The ecosystem is broad, so the menu has to help people choose rather than just display everything.
- Short descriptors reduce ambiguity and make unfamiliar categories easier to judge.
- Illustration can soften the drawer and make it feel less mechanical.
- A curated menu surface feels more trustworthy than a wall of equally weighted links.
