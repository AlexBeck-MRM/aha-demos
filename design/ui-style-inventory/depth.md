# Depth

## Direction
Use depth to signal action and structural change, not to decorate everything. Soft elevation should pull forward buttons, cards, tabs, carousels, and state-changing panels while the rest of the page stays calm and bright.

## Reasoning
- Depth helps people read what is interactive first.
- It also marks shifts in state, grouping, and hierarchy without adding more labels or colour.
- Subtle tangibility is one of the clearest ways to move the experience away from flat institutional UI.
- If the effect gets glossy, dark, or heavy, trust drops.

## Guardrail
Avoid dark, muddy shadows or exaggerated glassmorphism.
