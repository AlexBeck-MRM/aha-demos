# Search

## Direction
Give search an always-available role in the interface. Keep the field visibly open in the header, and let key pages use a larger search surface with placeholder language that tells people what kinds of questions or tasks it can handle.

## Reasoning
- Search is one of the clearest usefulness signals in a broad content ecosystem.
- An already-open field removes an unnecessary step and invites immediate action.
- Helpful placeholder language can widen expectations beyond literal keyword search without overselling AI.
- Search should feel like a primary guidance path, not a tucked-away utility.

## Open questions
Should search live in the hero, the nav, or both for key views?
