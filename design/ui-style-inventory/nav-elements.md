# Nav Elements

## Direction
Use floating navigation with clear separation from the browser edge. Keep the bar bright, compact, and tactically clickable, with one strong primary CTA and calm supporting utilities.

## Reasoning
Floating nav immediately makes the site feel more designed and more ownable. It also softens the institutional feel that edge-attached bars often create.

## Route emphasis
Route A can feel slightly softer and more editorial. Route B can feel slightly cleaner and more engineered.
