# Trust Cues

## Direction
Integrate trust through credible content signals, clinician or community context, clear pathways, and stable interface behavior. Trust should be embedded, not pasted on as badges.

## Reasoning
AHA credibility is one of the strongest assets in the system. Trust feels stronger when it is woven into the experience rather than stacked as proof clutter.

## Guardrail
Avoid overloading pages with institutional logos, statistics, and boilerplate at the expense of readability.
