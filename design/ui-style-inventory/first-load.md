# First Load

## Direction
Protect the first screen from stacked interruptions. The opening view should establish meaning, navigation, and trust quickly, with cookie handling and donation prompts staying present but not blocking people from engaging with the page.

## Reasoning
- The first view sets confidence, and too many competing prompts immediately raise friction.
- People need agency in how they move toward content, support, or donation rather than being forced through multiple dismissals.
- A lighter cookie treatment can preserve legal and practical needs without obscuring the page.
- We still need to decide how assertive donation messaging should be before it starts hurting the first impression.

## Open questions
How much campaign or supporting content belongs above the first major scroll?
