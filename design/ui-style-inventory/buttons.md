# Buttons

## Direction
Primary buttons should feel chunky, rounded, softly elevated, and compelling to click. Secondary buttons should retain clarity and tactility without competing for attention.

## Reasoning
Buttons are one of the signature expressions of the site's subtle skeuomorphism. Strong button design helps the entire ecosystem feel more alive and actionable.

## Route emphasis
Route A can allow slightly warmer, softer button presence. Route B can keep the same tactility with a cleaner edge definition.
