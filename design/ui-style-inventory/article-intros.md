## Direction
Article intros should establish context and credibility quickly. Pair a clear topic signal, a decisive headline, brief authorship or role context, and a short summary so people know why the piece matters before they commit to a long read.

## Reasoning
- Health articles need fast orientation before they need atmosphere.
- Authorship and role context help readers judge credibility quickly.
- A concise summary reduces the risk of dropping into a long page without enough context.
