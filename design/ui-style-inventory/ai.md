# AI

## Direction
Treat AI as explicit, guidance-oriented assistance tied to real jobs such as search, triage, or decision support. Keep it transparent, easy to dismiss, and clearly subordinate to the user.

## Reasoning
AHA can use AI to feel more useful, but novelty without clarity would weaken trust. This section should stay focused on responsible utility, not speculative interface theater.

## Open questions
Should AI live primarily inside search and guidance pathways, or should AHA ever present it as a standalone assistant surface?
