# AI

## Direction
Treat AI as explicit support for real jobs such as search, question-answering, or triage. It should feel transparent, easy to dismiss, and clearly subordinate to trusted AHA guidance rather than becoming the experience itself.

## Reasoning
- AI is only valuable here when it makes trusted guidance easier to reach or use.
- Clear boundaries matter because novelty without explanation weakens confidence fast.
- AHA should coexist with the tools people already use, not pretend they do not exist.
- This section still needs clearer decisions on which jobs deserve AI rather than a simpler interface pattern.

## Open questions
Should AI live primarily inside search and guidance pathways, or should AHA ever present it as a standalone assistant surface?
