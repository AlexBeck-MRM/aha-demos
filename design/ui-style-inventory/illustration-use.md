# Illustration Use

## Direction
Use illustration as a branded support layer inside menus, cards, promos, and explainer surfaces where photography would feel too literal or too heavy. Keep scenes simple and warm, with colour arriving through one or two broad focal areas rather than many small accents.

## Reasoning
- Illustration works best when it has a clear job: explain, soften, guide, or add gentle character.
- It gives the system more ownership and warmth without asking photography to do every expressive job.
- Broader colour permission makes sense here, but the palette still needs discipline.
- AHA red is strongest as a focal block, not as lots of small repeated details.
