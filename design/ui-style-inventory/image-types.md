# Image Types

## Direction
Define image roles before defining image treatment. Distinguish between documentary trust photography, family and community storytelling, clinical or expert reassurance, illustration-led explanation, and branded breakout moments.

## Reasoning
This section should help the system choose the right visual job before deciding crop, density, or styling. It prevents every image from trying to carry the same emotional and informational load.

## Guardrail
Do not use image type as a substitute for route direction. Route expression still depends on treatment, placement, tone, and surrounding UI.
