# Styleframe 02: Website Ecosystem

## Purpose
Prove that the direction scales into a useful, content-rich system rather than only working in hero moments.

## Required ingredients
- homepage hero
- mega menu
- topic or content band with tabs
- cards or carousel
- top of an article or resource page
- campaign or initiative banner
- donate or contribution module
- search or discovery module
- community or volunteer moment
- one memorable branded moment

## Direction
This frame should feel operational, useful, and credible while still carrying the same visual conviction established in Styleframe 01.

## What to emphasize
- hierarchy across many module types
- clear navigation and discovery behavior
- content density that still feels curated
- trust cues integrated into the system, not bolted on

## What not to do
- flatten the frame into a sterile dashboard
- let utility erase warmth
- use repeated red accents as a substitute for hierarchy
