# Styleframe 01: Brand Presence

## Purpose
Show the brand in action through a few decisive moments rather than trying to explain the whole website.

## Required ingredients
- dominant homepage hero
- navigation or mega-menu signal
- small number of content cards
- editorial or article-intro moment
- utility or action module
- one memorable branded detail

## Direction
This frame should establish emotional clarity, brand behavior, and the felt quality of the site. It is the proof that AHA can look ownable, warm, and digital-first without losing trust.

## What to emphasize
- hero composition
- signature button behavior
- floating navigation
- image treatment
- one surprising but disciplined detail

## What not to do
- turn the frame into a full sitemap
- overload it with every content type
- rely on generic stock-photo energy
