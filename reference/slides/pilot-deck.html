Market Context

TN-03 Split Narrative

AI adoption is accelerating faster than operating models

Marketing teams are adopting tooling rapidly, but governance, handoffs, and measurement are lagging. This creates delivery speed in the short term and inconsistency in the medium term.

The near-term opportunity is to establish a repeatable operating rhythm: scoped experiments, clear ownership, and explicit quality gates between strategy, content, and activation.

Key implication

Without a shared execution model, productivity gains are uneven and difficult to scale.

What this means in practice

• Standardize briefing and approval handoffs. • Align campaign planning to a single weekly cadence. • Introduce metric ownership for each workstream.

Expected outcome

Faster cycle times with fewer quality regressions.
