Healthy Living Guide System

Directional hub, evergreen guides, linked resources

This removes the intermediate pillar hub. The Super Hub routes people into the right guide. Each guide behaves like a structured course or handbook. Articles, tools, recipes, videos, and infographics support specific chapters.

1. Super Hub Helps people choose the right guide through intent and health-goal routing.
2. Evergreen Guide The main learning destination. Structured like a course, book, or handbook.
3. Linked Support Articles, recipes, tools, videos, and infographics sit inside guide chapters.
