Healthy Living page system

How guide pages, chapters, and supporting pages should show up

The topic page should give people a clear overview of the whole guide and a fast way to jump to the right information. Supporting pages stay findable, but they no longer define the primary structure.

Canonical chaptersThe guide is the topic home. It should read like a handbook, not a campaign archive.
Fast findPeople can jump by goal, symptom, barrier, chapter, or content type without reading linearly.
Curated supportArticles, recipes, tools, and infographics appear inside the chapter where they help.
Clear recoverySubpages always show the guide and chapter they belong to, plus a route back.
