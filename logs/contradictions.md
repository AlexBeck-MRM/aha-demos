# Contradictions

## 2026-04-11

### CONTRA-001
Tension: `Less is more` vs a very broad content and engagement ecosystem.

Why it matters:
The future site needs calm and clarity, but it also needs to expose significant breadth.

Resolution direction:
Use stronger hierarchy, search, navigation, and modular disclosure rather than trying to reduce the ecosystem to a sparse brochure.

### CONTRA-002
Tension: strong brand equity in red vs current stakeholder fatigue with copy-paste red usage.

Why it matters:
Red is a signature asset, but overuse makes the experience feel flat and repetitive.

Resolution direction:
Use red strategically and rely more on form, depth, layout, and content rhythm for brand distinctiveness.

### CONTRA-003
Tension: `source of truth` authority vs `trusted companion` warmth.

Why it matters:
AHA must remain the most credible guide while feeling more human and relational.

Resolution direction:
Design for expert clarity with more human framing, supportive copy, and less institutional coldness.

### CONTRA-004
Tension: personalization ambition vs low current data and operational maturity.

Why it matters:
The brain cannot assume deeply personalized experiences as the default near-term reality.

Resolution direction:
Prioritize strong generic guidance, search, and pathways first; treat deeper personalization as a staged capability.

### CONTRA-005
Tension: need for flexible templates vs need for governance and editor usability.

Why it matters:
Visual freedom without template discipline will recreate current content inconsistency.

Resolution direction:
Favor a constrained but expressive template system with better naming, documentation, and preview behavior.
